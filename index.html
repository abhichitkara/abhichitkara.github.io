Power Aware Routing

Power Aware Routing Algorithm for MANETs in Disaster Recovery

Date:  May 2018

Category:  Research

Client:  Mr. Supratik Banerjee

Video Report

I worked on this Research and Development project, under Professor Supratik Banerjee at NIIT University, Neemrana. The aim of this research is to provide a power aware routing algorithm for MANET’s used in the form of smart badges during disaster relief. These smart badges are used to locate the survivors of structural collapse. They’re ideal for the situation due to low data rates, power consumption. We propose to modify the AODV protocol as per our need and make it power aware.
